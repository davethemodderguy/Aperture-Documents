II. OVERVIEW

The External Air Defense System (EADS) is a distributed network of high-caliber interceptor platforms mounted along the exterior superstructure of the airborne Blacksite. Originally engineered for sub-surface deterrence, the system has undergone full structural and functional redesign to accommodate high-altitude operational demands.

The EADS ensures secure aerial logistics, maintains perimeter integrity, and neutralizes incoming threats within Blacksite-controlled airspace.

Initial deployment followed increased congestion and hostile interception activity in surrounding atmospheric corridors, where ██████████ and unauthorized craft began targeting supply routes, likely drawn by sustained ████████████ emissions.
