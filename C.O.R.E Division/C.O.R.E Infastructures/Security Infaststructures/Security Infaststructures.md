**C.O.R.E SECURITY INFRASTRUCTURE — DOSSIER**  
**Classification: Internal / Restricted**

---

### I. PURPOSE

**C.O.R.E Security Infrastructure** encompasses all systems responsible for **defense, control, and enforcement** within Aperture facilities.

These systems ensure that:

- Threats are detected immediately
- Unauthorized access is prevented
- Incidents are contained before escalation

Security Infrastructure is not passive.

It is designed to **act, respond, and neutralize**.

---

### II. CORE SYSTEMS

---

#### **1. SURVEILLANCE NETWORK**

- Full-spectrum cameras (visual, thermal, motion tracking)
- Continuous monitoring across all sectors
- Blind spot minimization protocols

**Function:**  
Provides real-time awareness of all activity within the facility.

---

#### **2. ACCESS CONTROL SYSTEMS**

- Biometric authentication
- Clearance-level access restrictions
- Identity verification checkpoints

**Function:**  
Ensures only authorized personnel can access restricted areas.

---

#### **3. AUTOMATED DEFENSE SYSTEMS**

- Turrets and anti-personnel units
- Environmental countermeasures
- Hazard deployment systems

**Function:**  
Engages and neutralizes threats without delay.

---

#### **4. LOCKDOWN & CONTAINMENT CONTROLS**

- Blast doors and sector sealing systems
- Emergency isolation protocols
- Controlled access shutdowns

**Function:**  
Prevents spread of breaches and secures compromised zones.

---

#### **5. INTERNAL RESPONSE INTEGRATION**

- Coordination with security teams
- Deployment support for Overseers and Juggernauts
- Rapid-response routing systems

**Function:**  
Ensures efficient deployment of personnel during incidents.

---

#### **6. EXTERNAL DEFENSE SYSTEMS (EADS)**

- Long-range defensive weaponry
- Perimeter protection systems
- Anti-air and external threat deterrence

**Function:**  
Protects facilities from outside interference or attack.

---

#### **7. AI OVERSIGHT — LUCY AI**

- Real-time threat analysis
- Automatic protocol activation
- System-wide command override

**Function:**  
Acts as the central intelligence coordinating all security responses.

---

### III. OPERATIONAL ROLE

C.O.R.E Security Infrastructure ensures:

- Immediate detection and response to threats
- Enforcement of facility control at all times
- Containment of both internal and external risks

During incidents, it is responsible for:

- Lockdowns
- Threat neutralization
- Personnel restriction and control

---

### IV. AUTHORIZATION LEVEL

Security systems are authorized to:

- Override standard access permissions
- Enforce compliance
- Use lethal force when required

---

### V. SYSTEM DEPENDENCY

Security Infrastructure depends on:

- General Infrastructure for power and system support
- AI systems for coordination and decision-making

Failure results in:

- Loss of control
- Unchecked breaches
- Facility compromise

---

### VI. FINAL NOTE

C.O.R.E Security Infrastructure does not wait for orders.

If something becomes a threat—

**it is already responding.**
