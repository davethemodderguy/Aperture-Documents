### **SYSTEM STRUCTURE**

A.M.P.S is divided into **three primary operational categories**:

---

## **[1] FOOD SYSTEM — “SUSTAINMENT GRID”**

A closed-loop agricultural network responsible for feeding all Blacksite personnel.

**Components:**

- Controlled-environment greenhouses
- Subterranean farming sectors
- Automated irrigation and nutrient cycling systems

**Operational Notes:**

- All food is **organically grown**
- No synthetic or artificial replication methods are permitted

> **Directive:**  
> _“We will NEVER 3D print food or involve anomalies.”_

This ensures:

- Biological stability
- Elimination of anomalous contamination risk
- Long-term sustainability

---

## **[2] AIR SYSTEM — “ATMOSPHERIC PURIFICATION NETWORK”**

A planetary-scale filtration system responsible for maintaining breathable air within the Blacksite.

**Process Flow:**

1. External air is **drawn from Earth’s atmosphere**
2. Air is routed through **high-density filtration chambers**
3. All molecules **excluding Hydrogen and Oxygen** are:
    
    > **Disintegrated at the molecular level**
    

**Output:**

- Highly purified, breathable atmospheric composition

**Capabilities:**

- Removes toxins, pollutants, and foreign particles
- Neutralizes airborne contaminants entirely

---

## **[3] LIFE SYSTEM — “MEDICAL CORE”**

The **hospital infrastructure** of the Blacksite.

**Functions:**

- Injury treatment
- Surgical operations
- Recovery and stabilization
- Personnel health maintenance

**Integration:**

- Directly linked with all other A.M.P.S subsystems
- Acts as the **final failsafe** in preserving human life
