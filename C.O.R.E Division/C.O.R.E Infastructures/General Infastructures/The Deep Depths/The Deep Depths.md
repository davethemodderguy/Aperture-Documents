II. OVERVIEW

The Blacksite Quarry, designated Deep Depths, is a large-scale underground excavation network utilized by Aperture Industries for the extraction and processing of raw materials required for core manufacturing operations.

The quarry supports the majority of Aperture’s material supply chain, functioning as the primary source of inputs for machinery production, armor fabrication, and non-anomalous weapons systems.
