---

### I. PURPOSE

**C.O.R.E General Infrastructure** encompasses all systems required to maintain the **baseline operation and sustainability** of Aperture facilities.

These systems ensure that blacksites remain:

- Powered
- Habitable
- Functional

They do not directly engage threats—but without them, no response is possible.

### III. OPERATIONAL ROLE

C.O.R.E General Infrastructure ensures:

- Continuous facility operation
- Stability during emergency conditions
- Support for all divisions and systems

During incidents, it is responsible for:

- Power redistribution
- Environmental stabilization
- System continuity

---

### IV. LIMITATIONS

- Does not engage threats directly
- Relies on Security Infrastructure for protection
- Vulnerable if not properly maintained or defended

---

### V. SYSTEM DEPENDENCY

All Aperture operations depend on these systems.

Failure results in:

- Loss of containment support
- Breakdown of security systems
- Facility-wide instability
