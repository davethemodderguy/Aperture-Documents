II. OVERVIEW

The Central Operations & Research Executive (C.O.R.E) serves as the primary authority responsible for the identification, acquisition, containment, and study of anomalous entities and phenomena within Aperture Industries’ jurisdiction.

C.O.R.E operates as a sealed, high-security division tasked with maintaining control over all non-standard assets deemed hazardous, valuable, or strategically significant to corporate interests.

All operations conducted under C.O.R.E authority are classified and restricted to personnel with appropriate clearance.

---

III. PRIMARY FUNCTIONS

C.O.R.E is responsible for the following operational domains:

1. Acquisition

- Detection and retrieval of anomalous entities  
      
    
- Field operations and capture protocols  
      
    
- Transfer of assets to containment facilities  
      
    

2. Containment

- Design and maintenance of containment systems  
      
    
- Breach prevention and response protocols  
      
    
- Long-term stabilization of entities  
      
    

3. Research & Experimentation

- Behavioral and structural analysis of entities  
      
    
- Controlled testing under regulated conditions  
      
    
- Development of applied technologies derived from anomalies  
      
    

4. Neutralization

- Termination or suppression of entities deemed uncontrollable  
      
    
- Deployment of specialized weaponry and countermeasures  
      
    
- Emergency purge authorization protocols  
      
    

5. Internal Security

- Protection of C.O.R.E facilities and personnel  
      
    
- Enforcement of clearance levels and information control  
      
    
- Response to internal threats, sabotage, or compromise
