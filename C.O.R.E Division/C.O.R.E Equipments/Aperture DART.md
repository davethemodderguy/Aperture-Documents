I. DESIGNATION

Weapon ID: A-DART  
Codename: “D.A.R.T” (Deployable Adaptive Reactive Tool)  
Type: Multi-Mode Micro Explosive Device

---

II. OVERVIEW

The Aperture D.A.R.T is a compact, high-yield explosive device concealed within a standard ink pen reservoir. Designed for covert operations, the system provides extreme destructive capability within a low-profile form factor, making it ideal for:

- Breaching operations
    
- Retcon missions
    
- Espionage and infiltration
    

The device’s primary safety mechanism is its removable cap, preventing accidental activation.

---

III. PAYLOAD SYSTEM

The D.A.R.T utilizes micro-compressed nitro powder as its explosive core.

Payload Range:

- Minimum: 20g
    
- Maximum: 25g
    

Conversion Ratio:

- 1g Nitro Powder = 1000kg Gunpowder Equivalent
    

Result:

- Even minimum payload yields catastrophic explosive output
    
- Maximum payload exceeds conventional tactical explosive thresholds
    

---

IV. ACTIVATION MECHANISM

Upon removal of the safety cap:

- The bottom actuator becomes active
    
- User can cycle operational modes via click input
    

---

V. OPERATIONAL MODES

---

### 1. MISSILE MODE

Activation:

- Remove safety
    
- Grip base firmly
    
- Yank forward at high velocity
    

Function:

- Mid-air booster activates
    
- Sustained propulsion for up to 30 minutes
    

Impact Behavior:

- Detonates upon contact
    
- Can pierce thin materials before detonation
    

Failsafe:

- Auto-detonates after 50 minutes airborne or inactive
    

Use Case:

- Long-range precision strikes
    
- Mobile or distant targets
    

---

### 2. BREACHING CHARGE MODE

Activation:

- Tap bottom once
    
- Attach device to target surface (recommended: door hinge)
    
- Tap main body to initiate countdown
    

Function:

- Focuses explosive force into a single directional output
    

Capabilities:

- Efficient structural compromise
    
- Minimal collateral spread
    

Use Case:

- Door breaching
    
- Controlled entry points
    

---

### 3. GRENADE MODE

Activation:

- Hold base actuator
    
- Throw device
    

Function:

- Initiates 5-second countdown upon release
    
- Detonates on:
    

- Timer expiration
    
- Immediate impact (tip contact)
    

Yield:

- Equivalent to ~250kg dynamite
    

Use Case:

- Close-quarters combat
    
- Area denial
    

---

VI. CUSTOMIZATION PARAMETERS

The D.A.R.T system supports modular configuration:

- Payload Adjustment (20g–25g nitro core)
    
- Fuel Modifications (Missile mode duration/output)
    
- Mode Prioritization (pre-configured cycling order)
    

---

VII. SAFETY CONSIDERATIONS

- Safety cap must remain secured until deployment
    
- Mishandling may result in:
    

- Premature detonation
    
- Operator fatality
    

- Device is:
    

- Highly unstable post-activation
    
- Not recoverable after arming
    

---

VIII. STRATEGIC SIGNIFICANCE

- Combines stealth, adaptability, and extreme yield
    
- Enables operatives to carry multi-role explosives without detection
    
- Highly effective in asymmetric and covert warfare
