---

I. OVERVIEW

Warcaskets are the standard-issue combat armor systems utilized by Aperture Industries personnel. Despite their designation, Warcaskets serve as both the most basic and most advanced form of operator protection, integrating defensive plating, mobility enhancement, and energy-based augmentation into a unified platform.

Each Warcasket consists of:

- Reinforced Helmet Unit
    
- Chestplate / Vest Core
    
- Leggings Assembly
    

All systems are powered by an internal energy core, enabling both baseline functionality and variant-specific capabilities.

---

II. BASELINE SYSTEM FEATURES

All Warcasket variants are equipped with the following:

- Exoskeleton Frame:  
    Enhances physical strength and load-bearing capacity
    
- Composite Armor:  
    Titanium and Kevlar layered with █████████-grade armor plating
    
- Movement Enhancement Boots:  
    Increased speed, stability, and impact resistance
    
- Integrated Visor System:  
    Provides tactical overlays, environmental filtering, and visual clarity
    

---

III. CRITICAL SECURITY PROTOCOL

AUTOMATED SELF-TERMINATION SYSTEM

In the event that the wearer is:

- Incapacitated
    
- Downed
    
- Confirmed deceased
    
- Deemed “unable to combat”
    

The Warcasket will initiate a countdown sequence.

Upon completion:

- The suit will detonate, destroying:
    

- The wearer
    
- The suit
    
- All recoverable components
    

Purpose:

- Prevent reverse engineering
    
- Deny enemy acquisition
    
- Eliminate sensitive technology
    

Manual Override:

- Wearer may manually trigger detonation (kamikaze protocol)
    

Explosion Yield:

- Equivalent to 45× M67 Fragmentation Grenades
    
- Results in total annihilation within immediate radius
    

Note: All operators are fully aware of this protocol.
