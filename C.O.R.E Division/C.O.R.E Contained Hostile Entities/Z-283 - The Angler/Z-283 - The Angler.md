

**APERTURE INDUSTRIES  
C.O.R.E ENTITY DOSSIER — Z-283 “THE ANGLER”  
Classification: Internal / Level 5 Clearance Required**

---

I. ENTITY DESIGNATION

Primary Codename: The Angler  
Entity Identifier: Z-283  
Entity Type: Anomalous Non-Humanoid Organism (Aerial Smoke Mass)  
Current Status: Contained

---

II. OVERVIEW

Z-283 is an airborne anomalous entity manifested as a dense, semi-cohesive mass of black and blue smoke, bearing the visual resemblance of an anglerfish’s facial structure. Despite its amorphous composition, the entity exhibits consistent directional orientation behavior and autonomous locomotion.

First encountered during the initial exploration and excavation of the Hadal Blacksite within the Let-Vand zone (1962), Z-283 has remained in secure containment due to its unpredictable electromagnetic and biological effects.

---

III. PHYSICAL CHARACTERISTICS

- Composition: Dense smoke-like particulate mass (black/blue coloration)
    
- Apparent Feature: Fish-like facial formation resembling Melanocetus genus
    
- Structural Behavior:
    

- Maintains a fixed forward-facing orientation regardless of observer perspective
    
- Multi-observer viewpoint inconsistency persists under controlled observation
    

The cause of this orientation anomaly remains unexplained and is under ongoing theoretical review.

---

IV. LOCOMOTION & SENSORY SYSTEMS

Z-283 navigates via:

- Echolocation
    

- Emission: High-intensity acoustic scream
    
- Function: Environmental mapping and prey localization
    

This emission is sufficiently powerful to be perceived across large enclosed spaces and serves as a primary indicator of proximity.

---

V. ELECTROMAGNETIC EFFECTS

Z-283 generates electromagnetic disturbances during movement and hunting behavior.

Effects Include:

- Disruption of unsecured electronics connected to the Blacksite power grid
    
- Short-circuiting or malfunction of nearby systems
    
- Progressive degradation of electronic stability within affected zones
    

Two distinct emission tiers have been observed:

1. Broad Disruption Field

- Affects multiple rooms within an unmeasured radius
    
- Causes intermittent system failures and lighting instability
    

2. Focused Pulse

- High-intensity localized discharge
    
- Effective range: approximately █ by █ studs
    
- Capable of destroying unsecured electronics within its immediate vicinity
    

---

VI. FEEDING MECHANISM (THEORETICAL MODEL)

Current leading hypothesis suggests:

- Z-283 consumes biological entities by passing through them
    
- Interaction results in absorption of bioelectrical energy
    
- Victims are left as biologically intact but electrically inert husks
    

This absorbed bioelectricity is believed to:

- Sustain Z-283’s movement
    
- Recharge electromagnetic discharge capability
    
- Enable prolonged hunting activity
    

Autopsy Correlation:

- Examination of █ LR-P subjects indicates:
    

- Extensive neurological degradation
    
- Absence of external trauma
    
- Internal bioelectrical depletion consistent with the above model
    

---

VII. CONTAINMENT PROTOCOL

Z-283 is secured within a reinforced containment unit composed of:

- Primary Material: █████ reinforced composite
    
- Layering: 25 successive identical structural layers
    

Containment is designed to suppress electromagnetic leakage and prevent physical escape.

---

VIII. BREACH INDICATORS

Personnel should identify the following early warning signs:

- Flickering of lights and electrical instability
    
- Audible high-intensity scream
    
- Progressive blackout of nearby systems
    

These indicators typically precede direct entity traversal through an area.

---

IX. EMERGENCY RESPONSE GUIDELINES

In the event of a suspected breach:

- Avoid reliance on electronic systems
    
- Seek obscured or enclosed positions
    
- Maintain distance from known trajectory paths
    
- Utilize battery-powered lighting sources when available
    
- Proceed toward areas with verified operational power systems
    

Emergency Light Systems may fail or delay activation during electromagnetic disruption.

---

X. STRATEGIC UTILIZATION (MAD PROTOCOL)

Z-283 holds no direct economic or industrial value but is retained under Mutual Assured Destruction (MAD) protocols.

Protocol Directive:

In the event of hostile disclosure or exposure of Aperture Industries’ illicit operations:

- Containment unit is to be transported to the surface
    
- Unit is to be airlifted to the responsible external entity’s headquarters
    
- Upon arrival, the containment unit is to be released, resulting in impact-triggered containment failure
    

Outcome:

- Immediate release of Z-283 into the target environment
    
- No strategic concealment or mitigation objective
    
- Functionally classified as retaliatory deterrence
