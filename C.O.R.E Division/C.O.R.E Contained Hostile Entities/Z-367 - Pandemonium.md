

**APERTURE INDUSTRIES  
C.O.R.E ENTITY DOSSIER — Z-367 “PANDEMONIUM”  
Classification: Internal / Level 5 Clearance Required**

---

I. ENTITY DESIGNATION

Primary Codename: Pandemonium  
Entity Identifier: Z-367  
Entity Type: Anomalous Mass Entity (Biological Composite)  
Current Status: Contained (Heaven’s Blacksite)

---

II. OVERVIEW

Z-367 is a large-scale anomalous entity composed of an unidentifiable, self-sustaining mass of organic and polymer-like material. First discovered in 1962 within the Let-Vand zone near a hydrothermal vent during initial Hadal Blacksite excavation, the entity has since been classified as both a high-threat organism and a functional industrial asset under controlled conditions.

Following a containment breach during the APRT-60 Blacksite detonation, Z-367 was temporarily uncontained and reached the surface before being successfully re-secured at the Heaven’s Blacksite facility.

---

III. DISCOVERY & INCIDENT HISTORY

- 1962: Initial discovery during Hadal Blacksite expedition
    
- 1965: Territorial conflict triggered by expansion of Blacksite infrastructure
    

- Casualties: Over █ LR-P personnel
    
- Injuries: ███ personnel rendered non-operational
    

Containment Operation:

- Authorized by Director ████
    
- Method: Controlled baiting using LR-P personnel
    
- Outcome: Successful luring into reinforced tungsten enclosure
    

Z-367 was subsequently transferred into a dedicated containment chamber following stabilization.

---

IV. PHYSICAL CHARACTERISTICS

- Composition: Indeterminate biological mass
    
- Structure: Interwoven organic tissue with polymer-like elements and mixed materials
    
- Morphology: Non-static, continuously deforming mass
    

No consistent anatomical structure has been identified.

---

V. SENSORY & TRACKING SYSTEMS

Z-367 possesses multiple intraoral sensory organs resembling eyes.

Capabilities:

- Detection of minute movement
    
- Sensitivity to light variations
    
- Long-range target acquisition
    

These sensory nodes are distributed internally and allow for multi-angle environmental awareness despite lack of conventional sensory organs.

---

VI. BEHAVIORAL PROFILE

Target Acquisition:

- Upon detection, Z-367 locks onto a target and initiates pursuit
    
- Tracking persists until:
    

- Target is assimilated
    
- Environmental interference disrupts tracking
    

Pursuit Characteristics:

- Persistent and non-relenting
    
- Capable of navigating complex environments
    
- Does not disengage easily once pursuit begins
    

Obstacle Interaction:

- Physical barriers do not deter movement
    
- Repeated impact against obstructions continues until:
    

- Structural failure occurs
    
- Target is lost or unreachable
    

---

VII. FEEDING & METABOLISM

Z-367 demonstrates both scavenging and opportunistic predatory behavior.

Diet Includes:

- Biological matter (living or deceased)
    
- Carrion and human remains
    
- Inorganic materials:
    

- Gravel
    
- Salt
    
- Certain synthetic polymers
    

Ingestion Process:

- Internal chemical agents dissolve consumed material
    
- Assimilation occurs within the entity’s mass
    

Molecular analysis suggests a hybrid composition of decomposing organic matter integrated with polymer-like structures and trace foreign materials.

---

VIII. FUNCTIONAL APPLICATION

Under Overseer Werner’s assessment, Z-367 has been repurposed as a controlled waste processing asset.

Operational Role:

- Primary landfill system for Blacksite refuse
    
- Continuous ingestion of waste materials to maintain stability
    

Waste Handling Protocol:

- All personnel-generated refuse is directed to Z-367
    
- Non-processable materials:
    

- Stored at Dock #█
    
- Transported to surface via monthly cycles
    

Maintaining a consistent “feeding” schedule is considered essential to prevent agitation.

---

IX. CONTAINMENT STATUS

- Primary Location: Heaven’s Blacksite containment landfill
    
- Containment Type: Reinforced containment chamber integrated with waste processing infrastructure
    
- Material Composition: Tungsten-reinforced enclosure
    

Z-367 remains under continuous monitoring and controlled feeding protocols.

---

X. BREACH & EMERGENCY RESPONSE

In the event of containment instability:

Indicators of Agitation:

- Increased movement within containment
    
- Elevated activity during waste processing cycles
    
- Observable structural stress responses
    

Escalation Risk:

- Agitation is considered a precursor to potential breach attempts
    

Personnel Directives:

- Avoid visual contact at all costs
    
- Upon visual confirmation, evasion is considered nonviable
    
- Immediate relocation to reinforced bunkers or hardened facilities is mandatory
    

Fallback Measures:

- Standard lockers may provide limited protection; however, resistance against Z-367 is unverified and should only be used as a last resort
    

---

XI. INCIDENT NOTE — APRT-60 EVENT

Following the detonation of the APRT-60 Blacksite:

- Z-367 breached containment and reached the surface
    
- Entity was subsequently tracked and successfully recontained at Heaven’s Blacksite
    

---

XII. OPERATIONAL DOCTRINE

- Z-367 is treated as both a containment priority and a functional infrastructure component
    
- Stability is maintained through controlled feeding and environmental management
    
- Agitation levels are directly correlated with containment risk
    

Escalation events are to be treated as high-severity incidents requiring immediate administrative response.

---

Threat Classification: EXTREME (UNSTABLE IF UNMANAGED)  
Operational Role: WASTE PROCESSING / CONTROLLED ASSET
