### **OVERVIEW**

Z-90, designated **“Wall Dwellers,”** is a large arthropod-like ambush species capable of **perfect environmental integration** within walls, floors, and structural surfaces.

Originally encountered off-site, Z-90 colonies have since been **fully acquired and repurposed** by Aperture Industries for:

- Biological research
- Material studies
- **Nutritional production**

---

> **Not one specimen — an entire embedded colony**

- 110+ Z-90 instances captured

> **“Construction accident”**

### **BIOLOGICAL STRUCTURE**

Z-90 anatomy is **non-standard**:

- Lacks traditional organ systems
- Contains only:
    - Integumentary system
    - Digestive system

Comparison:

> **Closer to carnivorous plant than animal**

---

### **PHYSICAL DESCRIPTION**

- Height: ~7.4 studs
- Two massive **raptorial forelimbs** (full-body coverage)
- Pale, chalk-like exterior
- Completely conceals torso during dormancy

Primary function:

> **Camouflage + trapdoor mechanism**

---

### **CAMOUFLAGE SYSTEM**

Z-90 utilizes specialized structures known as:

> **“Torque Cells”**

These cells:

- Contain serrated, gear-like microstructures
- Function as **microscopic chainsaws**
- Secrete adaptive digestive acid

**Embedding Process:**

- Plates press into surface
- Rotational motion creates micro-fractures
- Acid dissolves material
- Entity merges seamlessly into structure

Time to embed:

> **Minutes**

---

### **ADAPTIVE MIMICRY**

- Chromatophores, iridophores, leucophores present
- Visual match includes:
    - Color
    - Texture
    - Reflectivity

**Result:**

> **Near-perfect environmental invisibility**

---

### **BEHAVIOR**

Z-90 operates as a **stealth ambush predator**

**Hunting Cycle:**

1. Remains dormant inside structure
2. Detects prey (mechanism unknown)
3. Emerges silently
4. Stalks target post-passage

If observed:

- Freezes immediately
- Uses forelimbs as visual cover
- May fake retreat → initiate secondary ambush

---

### **PERCEPTION ANOMALY**

> Detects being “perceived” through walls

Cause:

> **Unknown**
