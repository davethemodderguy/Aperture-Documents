I. ENTITY DESIGNATION

Primary Codename: Eyefestation  
Entity Identifier: Z-317  
Entity Type: Modified Biological Anomaly (Genetically/Experimentally Altered Marine Organism)  
Origin Species: Carcharhinus leucas (Bull Shark)  
Program Affiliation: B.U.P.W.M

---

II. OVERVIEW

Z-317 is a heavily modified bull shark subjected to experimental augmentation under the B.U.P.W.M program. The entity exhibits extensive physiological, neurological, and anomalous deviations from baseline species characteristics, including multi-site ocular proliferation, radiation emission, and limited telekinetic influence over nearby targets.

---

III. EXPERIMENTAL ORIGIN

Z-317 was developed as part of controlled biological enhancement trials. Initial outcomes produced a combination of expected adaptive traits and unintended systemic anomalies, resulting in a highly unstable organism with both cognitive and physiological divergence from baseline parameters.

---

IV. PHYSICAL ANOMALIES

Observed structural modifications include:

- Proliferation of additional ocular structures across the body
    
- Embedded eyes located within:
    

- Gills
    
- Stomach
    
- Esophagus
    

- Internal organ degradation attributed to radiation exposure
    
- Cranial deformation and structural irregularities
    

Ocular Characteristics:

- Luminescent green emission
    
- Cross-shaped pupils
    
- Distributed visual systems across internal and external anatomy
    

Radiographic imaging confirms widespread internal integration of ocular structures beyond conventional anatomical constraints.

---

V. NEUROLOGICAL PROFILE

Early-stage observations indicated:

- Increased forebrain mass
    
- Structural development analogous to primitive cerebral cortex features
    
- Cognitive capabilities exceeding baseline bull shark behavior
    

Documented traits included:

- Curiosity-driven proximity to researchers
    
- Sustained attention spans
    
- Problem-solving ability
    
- Memory retention and adaptive reasoning
    

Behavioral assessments suggested intelligence levels comparable to a human adolescent during early experimental phases.

---

VI. BEHAVIORAL TRANSITION

As mutations progressed:

- Behavior shifted from non-aggressive curiosity to withdrawal
    
- Eventually transitioned into hostile engagement patterns
    
- Increased avoidance of direct human interaction unless provoked or influenced by environmental conditions
    

---

VII. ANOMALOUS CAPABILITIES

Z-317 exhibits a limited form of telekinetic or cognitive influence characterized by:

Visual Compulsion Effect:

- Targets within proximity are compelled to maintain eye contact
    
- Motor control interference may occur in affected individuals
    

Constraints:

- Range-limited
    
- Intensity varies depending on proximity and exposure duration
    

Countermeasures:

- Breaking line of sight
    
- Introducing opaque visual barriers
    
- Avoiding direct visual engagement
    

---

VIII. RADIATION EXPOSURE

Z-317 emits hazardous radiation:

- Prolonged proximity results in severe physiological degradation
    
- Radiation appears to correlate with ocular anomalies and internal mutation processes
    
- Communication attempts in close range frequently resulted in acute health deterioration among personnel
    

---

IX. LURE & MIMICRY BEHAVIOR

Z-317 has demonstrated potential psychological manipulation traits:

- Projection of familiar or recognizable voices
    
- Auditory mimicry used to attract personnel
    
- Luring individuals toward containment or proximity zones
    

These behaviors increase risk of accidental exposure and engagement.

---

X. CONTAINMENT & TERMINATION HISTORY

- Supervisor Authorization: Euthanasia approved (October 18th, 2025)
    
- Containment deemed increasingly untenable due to anomalous effects and radiation hazards
    

---

XI. POST-LOCKDOWN EVENT

- At approximately 18:16 hours, Z-13 breached Z-317 containment
    
- Entity was released into the open ocean
    
- Z-317 remained within the Let-Vand zone rather than dispersing
    

Following the detonation of the APRT-60 Blacksite:

- Z-317 was exposed to the Black Dawn Protocol blast
    
- Entity was confirmed destroyed
