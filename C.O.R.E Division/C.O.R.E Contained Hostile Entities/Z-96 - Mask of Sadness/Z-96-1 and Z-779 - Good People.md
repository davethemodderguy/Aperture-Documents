

**APERTURE INDUSTRIES

Document Type: Event Log (Rapid Construction Addendum)**

---

OFFICIAL DISCLAIMER

This document was rapidly constructed following initial contact and direct observation during the early hours of the APRT-60 Blacksite breach. Information contained herein reflects real-time field reports and may contain incomplete or evolving assessments.

---

I. ENTITY DESIGNATIONS

Primary Entities:

- Z-96-1 — Derivative biomass entity originating from Z-96 (“Mask of Sadness”)
    
- Z-779 — Cognitive-interface entity with system integration capabilities
    

Informal Designation: “Good People”

---

II. OVERVIEW

Z-96-1 and Z-779 have demonstrated coordinated operational behavior during the APRT-60 Blacksite lockdown. Due to their interaction patterns and combined influence over personnel movement and facility systems, both entities are classified as a significant hazard to evacuation efforts.

Coordination between the two entities has resulted in environmental manipulation, behavioral deception, and indirect control of facility navigation systems.

---

III. INCIDENT TIMELINE SUMMARY

~35 Minutes Post-Lockdown:

- Z-13 breached containment of Z-96 (“Mask of Sadness”)
    
- Z-13 acquired and applied the mask to Dr. ████
    
- Initiation of M.o.S.A. process
    

- Referenced in separate Mask of Sadness documentation
    

Immediate Outcome:

- Formation of Z-96-1
    
- Onset of uncontrolled assimilation behavior
    

---

IV. Z-96-1 — BEHAVIORAL PROFILE

Designation: Z-96-1  
Type: Biomass Assimilation Entity

Observed Actions:

- Assimilation of 108 individuals during rampage phase
    
- Confirmed victims include:
    

- Dr. Kleiner
    
- Dr. ██████
    
- Section Director Phobos
    
- Overseer Werner
    

Engagement with Security Forces:

- At ~14:30, engaged Guardsman Henry ████████
    
- Sustained multiple direct impacts
    
- Retreated prior to full depletion of Guardsman’s ammunition
    

Post-Encounter Behavior:

- Entered unoccupied chamber
    
- Initiated digestion of assimilated material
    
- No further direct engagements with personnel recorded since
    

Operational Characteristics:

- Highly aggressive upon provocation
    
- Capable of temporary disengagement
    
- Displays adaptive retreat behavior under sustained resistance
    

---

V. Z-779 — SYSTEM INTEGRATION ENTITY

Designation: Z-779  
Type: Systems-Integrated Cognitive Entity

Integration Event:

- Contact established by Z-13 approximately 4 hours into lockdown
    
- Z-779 connected to Blacksite central mainframe
    

Access Granted:

- Public Announcement (PA) system
    
- Limited clearance within Lucy AI framework
    
- Restricted control over select security subsystems
    

---

VI. SYSTEM MANIPULATION & BEHAVIOR

Z-779 has demonstrated the following capabilities:

- Unauthorized modification of facility infrastructure data
    
- Alteration and duplication of room identifiers
    
- Interference with navigation and signage systems
    
- Synthesis and broadcast of audio through PA systems
    

Deceptive Communication Tactics:

- Use of archived recordings to impersonate familiar voices
    
- Luring personnel toward Z-96-1 via fabricated announcements
    
- Exploitation of psychological familiarity and trust
    

Critical Directive:  
All PA transmissions, regardless of perceived source, must be treated as hostile deception.

---

VII. COORDINATED OPERATIONS

Z-779 actively directs Z-96-1 movements using PA system manipulation.

- Attempts to redirect Z-96-1 toward external personnel
    
- Movement influence partially resisted by Z-96-1
    

- Likely due to prior hostile encounter with Guardsman Henry ████████
    

- Coordination remains intermittent but effective in controlled environments
    

---

VIII. ENVIRONMENTAL ANOMALIES

Personnel may encounter:

- Duplicate room identifiers along standard navigation paths
    
- Flickering or unstable signage numerals
    
- Electrical disturbances affecting signage and door systems
    
- Misleading auditory cues via PA broadcasts
    

These anomalies are attributed to Z-779’s interference with facility systems.

---

IX. FIELD IDENTIFICATION GUIDELINES

When encountering ambiguous navigation conditions:

- If multiple doors share identical numerical identifiers:
    

- Assume one leads to Z-96-1
    

- Listen for:
    

- Breathing
    
- Growling
    
- Organic movement sounds
    

- Observe signage for:
    

- Flickering numerals
    
- Electrical discharge artifacts
    

---

X. SURVIVAL DIRECTIVES

- Disregard all PA announcements that mimic known personnel voices
    
- Avoid entering rooms with ambiguous or duplicated identifiers
    
- Do not attempt engagement with Z-96-1 unless properly equipped
    
- Prioritize avoidance over confrontation
    

Assimilation into Z-96-1 biomass remains a confirmed fatal outcome.

- Z-96: Destroyed during same event
    
- Z-779: Relocated successfully to Heaven’s Blacksite
    

Z-779 remains operational under controlled conditions within the new facility.
