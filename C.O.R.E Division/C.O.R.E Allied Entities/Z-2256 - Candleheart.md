### **Z-2256 — Codename: “Candleheart”**

**Classification:** Allied Entity  
**Threat Level:** Conditional  
**Status:** Contained / Cooperative

---

### **OVERVIEW**

Z-2256, designated **“Candleheart,”** is a semi-translucent anomalous spirit exhibiting consistent benevolent behavior toward Aperture Industries personnel. Unlike most contained entities, Z-2256 voluntarily surrendered itself upon awareness that its uncontrolled presence was destabilizing localized reality fields.

Z-2256 is permitted limited free movement within designated Blacksite sectors under passive observation.

---

### **DISCOVERY**

Following multiple civilian reports across Japan describing a **magenta-colored spirit assisting individuals**, Aperture Industries deployed an **Anti-Paranormal Activity Unit** to investigate.

Key observations from reports include:

- Assistance provided inconsistently across individuals
- Complaints primarily originating from **those ignored by the entity**
- Subtle environmental distortions in areas of prolonged manifestation

Upon arrival at the last recorded sighting, operatives successfully located Z-2256.  
**No resistance was encountered.**

Z-2256 willingly entered containment under the condition that it be treated **“like a human.”** Request was approved.

---

### **PHYSICAL DESCRIPTION**

Z-2256 manifests as a **magenta, plush-like spirit entity** with the following characteristics:

- Soft, rounded body structure
- Short limbs with **elongated, drooping ear-like appendages**
- Minimalistic facial features, typically expressing calm or happiness
- A **faint heart-shaped marking** centered on the torso
- A **candle-like cranial structure on her head**, emitting a constant magenta “flame”

The emitted flame:

- Produces **no conventional heat**
- Generates a **low-level spatial distortion field**
- Remains stable under all observed conditions

Z-2256’s body appears **partially phased**, fluctuating between solid and intangible states at will.

---

### **ORIGIN (THEORETICAL)**

Z-2256 is believed to be the lingering spirit of **█████████████**, a 23-year-old Kyoto resident deceased in 2012 during a unresolved murder that remains a mystery even till today.

Verification remains inconclusive; however, behavioral patterns and emotional responses strongly support this hypothesis.

---

### **BEHAVIORAL PROFILE**

Z-2256 demonstrates:

- High empathy
- Cooperative intelligence
- Child-like emotional responses

**Mental Age:** ~12 years  
**Physical Manifestation Age:** ~21 years

Primary behaviors include:

- Assisting personnel with daily tasks
- Engaging in playful interaction
- Seeking physical affection (e.g., hugging)
- Passive observation of activities

Z-2256 shows **notable attachment** to designated individuals, particularly:

- **Star**
- **Stitch**
- **Dusekkar**
- **Cinder**
- **Charlie**

(Collectively referred to as **“Dawnbringers”**)

Attachment intensity is highest toward **Star**, likely due to nominal association.

---

### **ABILITIES**

Z-2256 possesses multiple anomalous capabilities:

- **Telekinesis**  
    Capable of manipulating objects with precision and minimal effort
- **Intangibility / Phasing**  
    Can pass through solid matter without resistance
- **Selective Solidity**  
    Able to become fully tangible for interaction
- **Environmental Perception**  
    Can observe through walls and enclosed spaces
- **Emotional Resonance**  
    Exhibits strong emotional influence on nearby individuals

---

### **INTERACTION PROTOCOL**

Personnel are permitted and encouraged to interact with Z-2256 under the following guidelines:

- Maintain **respectful and non-threatening behavior**
- Avoid emotional distress or hostility in proximity
- Physical contact is permitted (entity is stable when solid)
- Do not attempt containment escalation or restriction

Z-2256 is considered **safe under normal emotional conditions**.

---

### **CRITICAL WARNING**

**Under no circumstances is Z-2256 to experience emotional collapse, extreme negative emotional response or “heartbreak.”**

Observed projections indicate that a severe emotional destabilization event may result in:

- Rapid amplification of anomalous output
- Uncontrolled reality distortion
- Hostile or destructive behavioral shift

As stated by Operator Star:

> _“Worse than me.”_

This statement is classified as **EXTREME RISK INDICATOR**.
