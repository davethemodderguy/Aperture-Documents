**APERTURE INDUSTRIES**

---

**I. ENTITY DESIGNATION**

Primary Codename: **The Shapeshifter**  
 Entity Identifier: **Z-454**  
 Entity Type: Amorphous Shapeshifting Anomalous Mass  
 Operational Classification: **Allied / Research Asset / Non-Hostile**

---

**II. OVERVIEW**

Z-454 is a non-hostile, amorphous orange mass capable of advanced shapeshifting, including the mimicry of humans, objects, and anomalous entities. The entity demonstrates cooperative behavior with Aperture Industries personnel and has consented to controlled research and material extraction procedures.

---

**III. DISCOVERY HISTORY**

**Date:** ██/██/████  
 **Location:** ████████████ HQ, Stockholm

Z-454 was encountered during a joint conflict involving Aperture Industries and Urbanshade forces.

**Event Summary:**

* Z-454 breached containment during the assault  
* Recognized and recovered by **Operator ██████████**  
* Extracted from the conflict zone and transported to Aperture custody

---

**IV. BEHAVIORAL PROFILE**

Initial disposition:

* Distrustful and disobedient toward human interaction  
* Attributed to prior experimentation and torture conducted by Urbanshade

Post-recovery behavior:

* Significant behavioral shift following rescue  
* Displays:  
  * Loyalty toward Aperture personnel  
  * Playful and social interaction patterns  
  * Voluntary cooperation in research activities

Z-454 is frequently observed interacting within safe zones, particularly in proximity to **Operator ███████████** prior to their death.

---
