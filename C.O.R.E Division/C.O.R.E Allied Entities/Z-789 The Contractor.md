 Status: **ACTIVE (ALLIED ENTITY — NON-CONTAINED)**

---

**I. ENTITY DESIGNATION**

Primary Codename: **The Contractor**  
 Entity Identifier: **Z-789**  
 Former Designations: *The Quest Giver*, *The Trader*  
 Entity Type: Humanoid Anomalous Entity (Non-Hostile / Transactional)  
 Operational Classification: **Allied / Autonomous / Non-Containable (Conditional)**

---

**II. OVERVIEW**

Z-789, hereafter referred to as **Contractor**, is an anomalous humanoid female entity exhibiting transactional behavior centered around task assignment (“quests”) in exchange for material rewards.

The entity demonstrates cooperative tendencies with Aperture Industries personnel and currently operates within Heaven’s Blacksite without enforced containment.

---

**III. PHYSICAL DESCRIPTION**

* Apparent Age: \~20 years  
* Attire:  
  * Dark coat with yellow accents  
  * Fedora (consistent)  
* Hair: Purple (dyed or biological origin undetermined)

**Anomalous Trait:**

* A persistent shadow cast from the fedora obscures the entity’s eyes under all observed lighting conditions

---

**IV. DISCOVERY & INITIAL CONTACT**

Contractor was identified by Aperture Industries following multiple reported incidents of:

* Smuggling  
* Theft  
* Petty criminal activity

**Clarification:**

* Contractor was not responsible for these actions  
* Frequently misidentified as a “shady woman inside an arcade”

---

**V. PRE-CONTAINMENT ACTIVITY**

Location: **██████████ Arcade, USA**

Contractor’s behavior included:

* Assigning tasks (“quests”) to children and teenagers  
* Providing rewards upon completion:  
  * Keychains  
  * Toys  
  * Small items  
  * Tickets

This behavior led to the designation **“The Quest Giver”**

---

**VI. CAPTURE EVENT**

During an operation led by **Operator ███████████**:

* Objective: Non-escalatory containment per Director directive  
* Contractor issued a request:

“Find something on the roof for me”

**Notable Conditions:**

* Arcade structure had no roof access  
* Team complied by physically stacking personnel to reach the target

Upon completion:

* Contractor left a sign:  
  * *“Gone out of stock, Sorry kids”*  
* Entity surrendered voluntarily to Aperture Industries

---

**VII. POST-TRANSFER BEHAVIOR**

Upon arrival at **Heaven’s Blacksite**:

* Contractor expressed approval of the facility  
  * Described as a “perfect place for business”

**Immediate Actions:**

* Established a stand within **Sector-4-C Cafeteria**
