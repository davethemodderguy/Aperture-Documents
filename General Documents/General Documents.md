**APERTURE INDUSTRIES  
GENERAL ARCHIVE — INTRODUCTION  
Classification: Internal

---

### I. PURPOSE

The GENERAL Archive serves as the structural backbone of Aperture Industries’ documentation system.  
It exists to house information that is essential to operations, yet not confined to any single specialized division.

While C.O.R.E and N.O.S.T handle execution, containment, and exploration, GENERAL defines the environment in which those actions are possible.

---

### II. SCOPE DISTINCTION

Not all assets, personnel, or protocols fall within the jurisdiction of specialized divisions.

C.O.R.E is responsible for:

- Containment operations
    
- Research and experimentation
    
- Weapons and security systems
    

N.O.S.T is responsible for:

- Extraterrestrial domains
    
- Interdimensional environments
    
- Non-Earth entities and phenomena
    

However, many elements of Aperture Industries:

- Exist above these divisions
    
- Operate between them
    
- Or support both simultaneously
    

These elements cannot be logically confined to either structure.

---

### III. WHAT GENERAL CONTAINS

The GENERAL Archive includes:

- Administrative Authority  
    Executive personnel and corporate leadership that govern all divisions
    
- Facilities & Infrastructure  
    Physical sites, headquarters, and structural systems that house operations
    
- Corporate Doctrine & Policy  
    The rules, philosophy, and directives that guide Aperture as a whole
    
- Site-Wide Protocols  
    Emergency and operational procedures that apply universally, regardless of division
    

---

### IV. JUSTIFICATION

Segregating all data strictly into C.O.R.E or N.O.S.T would create operational blind spots.

Aperture does not function as isolated divisions—it functions as a unified system.

The GENERAL Archive ensures:

- Continuity between divisions
    
- Centralized authority and control
    
- Standardized response across all facilities
    

Without it, coordination would collapse under specialization.

---

### V. OPERATIONAL PHILOSOPHY

If C.O.R.E is the body,  
And N.O.S.T is the reach beyond,

GENERAL is the structure that keeps both from falling apart.
