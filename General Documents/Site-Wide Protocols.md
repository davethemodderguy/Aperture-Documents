# Site-Wide Protocols

**APERTURE INDUSTRIES**  
 **SITE-WIDE PROTOCOL DOSSIER — UNIVERSAL ALERT SYSTEM**  
 **Classification: Internal / All Personnel Mandatory**

---

### **I. OVERVIEW**

**Site-Wide Protocols** are universal directives enforced across all Aperture facilities and divisions, including **C.O.R.E** and **N.O.S.T**.

These protocols:

* Are broadcasted through the **PA System**  
* Must be **immediately recognized and obeyed** by all personnel  
* Override standard operational procedures when activated

Each protocol is triggered via a **designated keyword**, ensuring rapid response without ambiguity.

Failure to comply is considered a **critical violation**.
