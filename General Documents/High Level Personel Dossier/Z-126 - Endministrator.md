**Former Identity:** Administrator Charles █████████

---

### **OVERVIEW**

Z-126, designated **“Endministrator,”** is the post-mortem spiritual manifestation of former **Aperture Administrator Charles █████████**, currently retaining full cognitive function, memory, and administrative capability.

Unlike standard anomalous entities, Z-126 continues to **actively fulfill his original role** within the **Security and Protections Division**, making him one of the few confirmed cases of **post-death personnel continuity** within Aperture Industries.

---

### **EVENT LOG — APRT-60 DETONATION**

During activation of the **Black Dawn Protocol** (APRT-60 Blacksite detonation), Administrator Charles played a critical role in evacuation procedures.

Confirmed actions:

- Secured safe transfer of **Z-2**
- Assisted in extraction of **Lucy AI Data Core**
- Oversaw relocation of multiple high-value assets
- Personally guided personnel to escape pods

**Casualties prevented:** Multiple (exact number classified)  
**Notable survivor:** Director Lucious

Final recorded statement prior to detonation:

> _“May my grace forever be remembered.”_

Z-126 was presumed deceased following detonation.

---

### **POST-MORTEM DEVELOPMENTS**

**3 months after death**, a personal USB device belonging to Charles was recovered during inventory inspection.

Upon activation:

- A **spiritual entity manifested** within his preserved office space
- Form visually and structurally similar to **Z-2256 (Candleheart)**
- Identity confirmed as Administrator Charles

Cause of manifestation remains **undisclosed**.

**Primary theory:**

- Connection to **Astral Projection Initiative**  
    (Experimental separation of soul from physical body)

Z-126 has **refused to elaborate** on his condition.
