### 2. APRT-60

Codename: The Blacksite

- Location: ███████ Trench, ███████
    
- Coordinates: [REDACTED]
    

Description:  
An underwater containment facility constructed following the decommission of APRT-50.  
Built in proximity to Z-2, a designated anomalous zone within the Mariana Trench where, beyond 69███ km, crushing pressure exhibits photonic properties comparable to a contained light pool.

- Capacity: 1500 Instances
    
- Amenities:  
    AI Core Room  
    Reactor Room  
    Servers  
    Living Quarters  
    Administration Wing  
    Security Room  
    Central Command Office  
    Armory  
    Waste Disposal  
    Airlocks  
    Submarine Bay  
    Cafeteria  
    Greenhouse
    
- Status: TERMINATED
    
- Reason for Termination:  
    Catastrophic mass containment breach initiated by Z-13.  
    Critical assets were recovered via Expendable Protocol.  
    Facility was neutralized through nuclear detonation, resulting in termination of Z-13 and permanent closure of Z-2.
