To the point where she doesn’t care about herself anymore—not in the way people say it casually, but in a real, visible way. Not once.

More.

To drink water.

Basic things. Things she used to do without thinking. And even then, she won’t do it unless I tell her, unless I stay there, unless I make sure she actually follows through. It’s like she won’t take care of herself unless someone else makes it matter.
