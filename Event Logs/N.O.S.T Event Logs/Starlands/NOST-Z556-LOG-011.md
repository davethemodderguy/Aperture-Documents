## **ENTITY OVERVIEW**

Borah is a large, bear-like predatory organism native to Z-556 “Starlands.” Unlike Dawnbringers or other documented entities, Borah is classified as a **natural apex predator** within its environment and does not fall under the Z-classification system.

Observed characteristics include:

* Extreme physical strength and durability  
* High-speed pursuit capability despite size  
* Aggressive hunting behavior targeting all living organisms  
* No signs of intelligence-based negotiation or restraint

The entity operates purely on predatory instinct.

---

## **INCIDENT SUMMARY**

On ██/██/████, Borah breached a N.O.S.T-established gateway and entered APRT-70 without warning.

Upon entry, the entity immediately began attacking personnel within Sector 4-C.

Casualties escalated rapidly due to:

* Lack of initial countermeasures against non-classified Starlands fauna  
* High density of personnel within the sector  
* The entity’s speed and lethality

**Total confirmed fatalities:** 2,500+ personnel

All victims sustained extreme physical trauma consistent with large predator attacks.

---

## **ENGAGEMENT RESPONSE**

Z-314-S (Star Dawnbringer) and Z-314-ST (Stitch Dawnbringer) responded immediately upon breach detection.

Engagement lasted approximately **2 hours**.

Combat observations:

* Borah demonstrated resistance to standard and anomalous attacks  
* Both entities sustained repeated direct hits during engagement  
* Combat extended across multiple subsections of Sector 4-C

Final termination of Borah was achieved through combined effort. Exact killing method remains under review due to conflicting sensor data.
