## **EVENT SUMMARY**

Following the events of the attempted assassination on Z-314-S (Star Dawnbringer), a new anomalous manifestation occurred within APRT-70.

A structure described as a **residential garden space** formed above the Blacksite. The formation was not detected as a conventional spatial breach or construction event, but rather as a gradual **manifestation**, integrating seamlessly into existing spatial architecture.

This location has been identified as an extension of Z-314-S’s known anomalous domain, commonly referred to as her “garden.”

At the center of this garden, prior observations consistently recorded the presence of a singular anomalous object:

* A rose composed entirely of **green luminescent energy**  
* Semi-transparent, ghost-like in structure  
* No physical mass detected, yet spatially anchored

This object is now confirmed to have been a **pre-manifestation state**.

---

## **MANIFESTATION EVENT**

On the date of record, the central rose disappeared without measurable energy fluctuation or displacement.

In its place, a humanoid entity was found lying at the center of the garden.

Description upon discovery:

* Female humanoid form  
* Entire body composed of **green, spectral luminescent energy**  
* No visible physical injuries  
* No immediate hostile response

Present at the scene:

* Director Lucious  
* Z-314-S (Star Dawnbringer)  
* Z-314-C (Charlie Dawnbringer)  
* Z-314-ST (Stitch Dawnbringer)

Notably, Z-314-S displayed **no signs of alarm**, indicating possible prior awareness or familiarity with the manifestation.

---

## **ENTITY IDENTIFICATION**

Upon regaining consciousness, the entity identified herself as:

“Viridis, Goddess of Nature.”

This statement triggered immediate recognition from Z-314-C, Z-314-ST, and Z-314-S.

“Viridis” is historically referenced as a **battle archangel-class entity** involved in the **Great Starladian War**, specifically noted for direct engagement alongside or against high-tier Dawnbringer lineage entities.

---

## **MEMORY DISCREPANCY**

When questioned regarding her identity, past involvement, and historical events, the entity responded:

“I… I don’t remember.”

Observed condition:

* Partial or complete amnesia  
* Lack of recall regarding the Great Starladian War  
* No recognition of previously associated individuals beyond surface interaction

This contradicts existing Starlands historical records.

Cause of memory loss: **Unknown**

---

## **BEHAVIORAL PROFILE**

Post-manifestation behavior indicates:

* Non-hostile disposition  
* Calm and passive demeanor  
* Strong affinity toward plant life and environmental growth

The entity has demonstrated immediate capability in:

* Accelerating plant growth  
* Maintaining ecological balance within the garden  
* Interacting with Starlands flora at an advanced level

No offensive capabilities have been displayed during initial observation.

---

## **RELATION TO Z-314-S**

* No defensive or aggressive reaction  
* Implicit acceptance of the entity’s presence

This suggests a potential **pre-existing connection**, either conscious or instinctive.

Further investigation required.

Her presence introduces both potential risk and strategic value.
