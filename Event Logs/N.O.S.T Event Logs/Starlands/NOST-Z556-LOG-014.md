## **EVENT SUMMARY**

Approximately three months after the events documented in NOST-Z556-LOG-011, Z-314-S organized a social gathering within APRT-70, identified as a “tea party.” The event was initiated by the subject as both a gesture of gratitude and a form of apology toward facility personnel, primarily in relation to the prolonged environmental effects caused by her prior Frost-state manifestations.

Attendance included key personnel and entities: Z-314-C (Charlie Dawnbringer), Z-314-ST (Stitch Dawnbringer), Director Lucious, The Contractor, and Operator Linda. The event proceeded without incident and was noted to have a positive impact on overall morale within the facility.

Following the conclusion of the gathering, Z-314-S returned to her assigned quarters.

---

## **INCIDENT DESCRIPTION**

Shortly after the event, multiple attempts were made to establish communication with Z-314-S. No response was received.

Concern escalated when her room was confirmed to be locked and unresponsive to standard access protocols.

Operator Linda initiated an override using elevated clearance previously granted after the events of LOG-011, intended for emergency intervention in cases involving Z-314-S’s safety or psychological state.

Despite successful system override, response delay resulted in forced entry occurring after an unknown time gap.

Upon door access, Z-314-S was discovered in a critically injured state.

---

## **INJURY REPORT**

Observed injuries included:

* Gunshot wound to the neck  
* Multiple stab wounds across torso and limbs  
* Extensive blood loss

Despite the severity of injuries, the subject remained barely conscious. Facial expression was noted as stable, including the presence of a smile, inconsistent with expected trauma response.

No attacker was present at the scene.

---

## **MEDICAL RESPONSE**

Z-314-S was immediately transported to the ER Wing.

During examination, medical staff identified a critical complication:

All wounds were contaminated with an **anti-regeneration residue**, a substance specifically inhibiting accelerated healing and anomalous recovery processes.

Effects included:

* Suppression of natural Starladian regeneration  
* Interference with previously documented healing factors  
* Sustained vulnerability to otherwise survivable injuries

Estimated survival window upon arrival: **\~2 hours**

Emergency procedures were conducted under maximum priority.

Through combined medical intervention and anomalous support from Z-314-C, Z-314-S was stabilized and restored to survivable condition within the available timeframe.

However, complete removal of the residue was not achieved.

Trace amounts remain active within the subject’s system.

---

## **POST-INCIDENT CONDITION**

Z-314-S is currently:

* Conscious  
* Stabilized  
* Under continuous monitoring

Residual effects of the anti-regeneration compound are still present, with unknown long-term impact on her healing capabilities.

No immediate relapse has been observed, but recovery is considered incomplete.

---

## **SECURITY CONCERNS**

* Method of entry by hostile party remains unknown  
* No breach alerts were triggered during the attack  
* Weapon types indicate premeditated intent and knowledge of target physiology  
* Use of anti-regeneration substance suggests **advanced preparation and familiarity with Dawnbringer capabilities**

This incident is classified as a **targeted assassination attempt**.

Perpetrator(s): Unknown  
 Threat Level: Elevated

---

## **OBSERVATION DIRECTIVE**

Due to the similarity in severity and psychological implications to prior events (LOG-011), Operator Linda has been reauthorized as primary observer.

She will continue maintaining **personal journal logs** documenting:

* Z-314-S’s recovery  
* Behavioral changes  
* Potential delayed effects of residue exposure  
* Emotional and psychological stability

All entries are to be archived under the existing **Operator Journal Series**.

---

## **CONCLUSION**

Z-314-S survived a critical, targeted attack involving specialized anti-regeneration measures designed to bypass her natural resilience.

While immediate fatality was avoided, the presence of residual inhibitory compounds and the unknown identity of the attacker introduce ongoing risk.

The incident confirms the existence of hostile forces with both the intent and capability to neutralize high-level Starlands entities within Aperture-controlled environments.

Investigation and monitoring remain active.
