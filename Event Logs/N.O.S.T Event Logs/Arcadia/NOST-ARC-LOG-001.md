# NOST-ARC-LOG-001

**APERTURE INDUSTRIES**  
 **N.O.S.T DIVISION — EVENT LOG**  
 **Classification:** Internal / HIGH PRIORITY

---

**Event ID:** NOST-ARC-LOG-001  
 **Subject:** First Contact — Arcadia / Visitor “Shu Sakurai” (Part 1\)  
 **Location:** APRT-00 “Main Lobby”  
 **Timeframe:** \+5 Weeks Post-Arrival of “The Contractor”

---

**Summary:**  
 Following a period of five (5) weeks after the arrival of the entity designated **“The Contractor”**, a formal announcement was issued regarding the establishment of multiple external partnerships. Among the listed entities was a previously unknown celestial body designated **“Arcadia.”**

Upon inquiry, The Contractor provided a preliminary explanation regarding Arcadia:

*“It's a planet faraway from this solar system, approximately five hundred quintillion light years away from the edge of this universe. A planet that exists outside the boundaries of the universe, and is home to the most technologically advanced species, the Arcadians.”*

The Contractor further disclosed that Arcadian interest in **Aperture Industries** had been established following initial contact, and that a representative would be visiting the facility.

---

**Administrative Reaction:**  
 Director **Lucious Lucious** expressed immediate concern regarding infrastructural readiness and containment integrity:

*“WHAT, this place isn't finished building, and you invited a technologically advanced life form to give us a visit????”*

Despite objections, preparations were initiated under The Contractor’s direction.

---

**Pre-Contact Protocol Notes:**  
 The following behavioral and cultural guidelines were distributed prior to arrival:

* Do **NOT** initiate physical gestures (waving, handshakes).  
* Physical contact must be initiated **by the Arcadian**.  
* **Feline entities** are considered sacred. Presence of subjects such as **Star** or **Stitch** (in feline form) is encouraged.  
* Elevated vocal tones (yelling) are to be strictly avoided.

---

**Arcadian Physiological Notes (Provided Document):**

* Humanoid structure closely resembling humans.  
* Distinctive **tail** terminating in a **spherical tip**.  
* Tail functions include **signal interception** across all known frequencies.  
* **Azure skin pigmentation** and **azure ocular coloration**.

---

**Designated Visitor:**  
 **Name:** Shu Sakurai  
 **Title:** Prince of Arcadia

**Notable Mutation:**

* Presence of **cranial antennae**, each terminating in spherical ends analogous to Arcadian tails.  
* Mutation grants enhanced capability:  
  * **Emotion Detection** within a localized radius of presence.

---

**Preparation Phase:**  
 Total preparation time: **3 hours**

Final decision regarding arrival location concluded after \~20 minutes of deliberation:  
 → **Designated Site:** Main Lobby (APRT-00)

Coordinates were transmitted by The Contractor to the Arcadian point of origin.

---

**Arrival Event:**  
 After approximately **5 hours**, an anomalous spatial disturbance manifested within the Main Lobby:

* Formation of a **door-shaped portal**  
* Emission: **Azure luminescence**  
* Structural stability: Sustained, non-fluctuating

Moments following stabilization, a **single figure** emerged from the portal.

---

**Status:**  
 Contact initiated.  
 Entity fully materialized.
