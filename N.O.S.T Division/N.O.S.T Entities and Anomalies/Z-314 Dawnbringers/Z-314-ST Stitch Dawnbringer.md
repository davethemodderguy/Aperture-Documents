## **GENERAL OVERVIEW**

Z-314-ST is the third confirmed instance of the Z-314 classification and is identified as the older sibling of Z-314-S (Star Dawnbringer).

The entity presents as a humanoid Starladian child, approximately ten years of age. However, his physiology deviates significantly from standard Dawnbringer structures due to extensive post-traumatic reconstruction.

Initial manifestation occurred via rift entry at APRT-70. Upon arrival, the entity appeared in a feline form consistent with known Dawnbringer transformations, displaying white fur, wings, and a halo. Distinct anomalous features included visible glowing stitching embedded across the body.

Following stabilization, the entity transitioned into a humanoid form while retaining these stitched structures.

---

## **PHYSIOLOGICAL CONDITION**

Z-314-ST’s most defining characteristic is the complete reconstruction of his left side.

According to the entity, the left arm, leg, and ear were destroyed in a forge-related explosion within Starlands. Reconstruction was performed by Z-314-C using an anomalous method that forces non-living biological material to function as living tissue.

As a result:

* Reconstructed areas are functional but not biologically normal  
* Tissue does not fully regenerate or sustain itself naturally  
* Structural degradation occurs over time

This creates a continuous need for maintenance.

---

## **SELF-MAINTENANCE CAPABILITY**

Z-314-ST performs all structural repairs manually.

The entity carries tools at all times, including needles, thread, and precision instruments. Observations confirm:

* Self-repair can be completed within seconds  
* Procedures are performed with high precision  
* No external assistance is required  
* Maintenance occurs frequently

Pain response is reduced or selectively suppressed. The entity shows minimal distress during repair procedures.

---

## **CAPABILITIES & SPECIALIZATION**

Z-314-ST identifies as a master blacksmith trained within Starlands.

**Primary capabilities include:**

* Precision forging of weapons and tools  
* High material control and balance optimization  
* Production of equipment used by other Dawnbringers (including Z-314-C)

The entity is typically observed carrying a sealed katana-type weapon. No hostile usage has been recorded during current operations.

---

## **ANOMALOUS TRAITS**

* **Stitched Physiology:** Glowing embedded stitching maintains structural cohesion  
* **Partial Non-Living Anatomy:** Sections of the body operate outside normal biological rules  
* **Halo Displacement:**  
  * Positioned offset to the right side of the body  
  * Interacts only with living tissue  
  * Does not attach to reconstructed (non-living) regions

The entity has described this effect as:  
 *“It sticks to what’s alive, and doesn’t stick to what’s dead.”*
