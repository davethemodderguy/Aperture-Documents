# Z-565-B "Lady Death"

“APERTURE INDUSTRIES — N.O.S.T DIVISION  
ENTITY DOSSIER: Z-565-B  
Codename: Lady Death  
Designation: Ferryman of the Banlands  
Classification: Restricted / Z-Class  
---

I. OVERVIEW

“Lady Death” is the current primary Ferryman operating within the Banlands (Z-565). She will hereafter be referred to as “Death.”

Death is responsible for the retrieval, transport, and routing of soul entities from **Z-565-A (Ocean of Souls)** toward designated outcomes: revival or treeification.

Despite holding a critical systemic role, Death demonstrates minimal engagement beyond required actions.

---

II. OPERATIONAL FUNCTION

Death’s primary responsibilities include:

* Retrieval of souls from the Ocean of Souls  
* Transport to processing points within the Banlands  
* Conditional revival of Aperture Industries personnel  
* Delivery of non-exempt souls for treeification

**Revival Protocol (Aperture Agreement):**

* Revival requires the expenditure of **Ferryman Tokens**  
* Tokens are issued and regulated by Aperture Industries  
* Without token compensation, Death will not perform revival

Advisory:

Personnel are strongly advised to avoid death. Repeated revival cycles result in increasing instability, both financially and physically.

---

III. BEHAVIORAL OBSERVATIONS

Death is largely non-communicative.

* All direct interview attempts: FAILED  
* Verbal interaction: Minimal to none  
* Compliance method: Transactional only

Post–Z-315 neutralization, Death’s activity has significantly declined.

Observed behavior:

* Prolonged inactivity at the **Lake of the Dead**  
* Passive observation of the Ocean of Souls  
* Delayed or absent response without token incentive

Notably:

* Tokens appear to have **no intrinsic value** to Death  
* After each completed revival, tokens are discarded into the Ocean

This behavior suggests symbolic, not economic, motivation.

---

IV. PSYCHOLOGICAL INDICATORS (THEORETICAL)

Due to lack of communication, all assessments remain speculative.

Primary theories:

1. **Residual Obligation**  
   Death continues her role out of attachment to prior duty structures.  
2. **Fatigue / Role Exhaustion**  
   Extended inactivity and disinterest indicate severe burnout.  
3. **Symbolic Compliance**  
   Acceptance of tokens may represent acknowledgment of external request rather than incentive.

Repeated rapid revivals of the same subject result in:

* Visible agitation  
* Increased physical force during revival process

Returned personnel may exhibit minor physical trauma.

---

V. RELATIONSHIP TO GOVERNING ENTITY

**The Devil (Banlands Administrator)** maintains a stable and cooperative relationship with Death.

* No recorded hostility between entities  
* Interactions (when observed) are informal and non-confrontational  
* Authority over Death appears limited or non-enforced

Despite systemic inefficiencies caused by Death’s inactivity, no direct punitive action has been taken by The Devil.

---

VI. SYSTEMIC IMPACT

Death’s reduced participation has resulted in:

* **Soul accumulation within Z-565-A (Ocean of Souls)**  
* Slowed processing rate of incoming souls  
* Decreased flow toward treeification systems

This has created partial imbalance within Banlands soul-cycle operations.

---

VII. INFRASTRUCTURE RESPONSE

In response to ongoing inefficiencies, The Devil has authorized external intervention.

Aperture Industries has been commissioned to develop:

**Ferryman Replacement System**

Status: IN DEVELOPMENT  
Estimated Completion: Late 2026

Objective:

* Stabilize soul retrieval and routing processes  
* Reduce reliance on Death’s voluntary participation  
* Restore full operational flow within Banlands systems

Death’s role post-deployment remains undefined.

---

VIII. DIRECTIVE NOTES

* All interaction with Death is to remain strictly transactional  
* Do not attempt extended communication  
* Avoid repeated revival cycles in short intervals  
* Do not interfere with her passive state at the Lake of the Dead

---

IX. STATUS

Operational State: PARTIALLY ACTIVE

Threat Level: LOW (NON-HOSTILE)
