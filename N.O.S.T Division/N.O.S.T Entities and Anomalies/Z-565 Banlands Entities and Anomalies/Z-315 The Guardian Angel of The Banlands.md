# Z-315 "The Guardian Angel of The Banlands"

APERTURE INDUSTRIES — N.O.S.T DIVISION  
 ENTITY DOSSIER: **Z-315**  
 Designation: *The Guardian Angel of The Banlands*  
 Classification: **Restricted / Z-Class**

---

## **I. OVERVIEW**

“*The Guardian Angel of The Banlands*” is an angelic entity discovered during the first expedition into The Banlands (Z-565) in 2006\.

The entity served as the primary defensive and obstructive force preventing further exploration into Banlands territory. It has since been captured during a subsequent assault and is currently contained and utilized for **Pureblood harvesting**.

As of this document’s status, no replacement Guardian has been appointed by The Almighty.

---

## **II. FIRST CONTACT (2006 EXPEDITION)**

During initial entry into The Banlands, expedition personnel were ambushed by the Guardian Angel.

**Engagement Summary:**

* Standard-issue small arms were deployed, including void-tipped ammunition  
* No measurable damage was observed on the entity  
* The Guardian demonstrated complete resistance to conventional weaponry  
* Behavioral patterns were interpreted as dismissive, including human-like gestures

Due to ineffective combat response and depletion of resources, expedition personnel initiated a tactical withdrawal back to Earth via the portal.

**Casualty Report:**

* Expedition member “Rivers” was killed during retreat  
* This remains the only recorded casualty from the initial incursion

---

## **III. RETALIATORY OPERATION**

Following the loss of personnel, Aperture Industries authorized a second, coordinated assault on the entity.

**Operational Strategy:**

* Luring the Guardian into an open engagement zone  
* Coordinated strike execution

**Execution:**

* Weapon utilized: *“Ironmark” Revolver* (originating from The Starlands)  
* Operator: Charlie (Z-314-C, *“The Guardian Angel of The Starlands”*)

**Outcome:**

* Successful decapitation of the Guardian Angel  
* Entity incapacitated and contained

The operation was conducted with approval from **The Devil** and **Z-314-C**.

---

## **IV. CURRENT STATUS**

Following containment, the Guardian Angel was transported to Earth.

* Physical state: Reduced to a barely conscious upper torso  
* Containment: Ongoing, under continuous monitoring  
* Utilization: **Pureblood harvesting via mechanical extraction apparatus**

The entity is maintained under conditions that allow for sustained viability despite continuous blood extraction.

---

## **V. PUREBLOOD OBSERVATIONS**

Analysis of the Guardian Angel’s blood—referred to as **“Pureblood”**—indicates the following:

* Individuals who consume Pureblood are **not directed to The Banlands (Z-565)** upon death  
* The substance is theorized to cleanse past sins from the soul  
* Effectiveness regarding actions committed after consumption remains unverified

Due to the inability to obtain confirmation from Heaven, all conclusions are considered **theoretical**.

---

## **VI. APPLICATION**

All resigning Aperture Industries personnel are issued measured doses of Pureblood.

**Purpose:**

* Prevent posthumous transfer to The Banlands  
* Ensure personnel are not subject to treeification or Ocean of Souls processing following death

This protocol ensures service-related activities do not result in unintended afterlife consequences.

---

## **VII. CONTINUOUS EXTRACTION SYSTEM**

The Guardian Angel is maintained under an active blood extraction apparatus.

**Observations:**

* The entity occasionally regains partial consciousness  
* Vocalizations in an unidentified holy tongue have been directed toward maintenance staff  
* Medical examinations (biopsy and carotid ultrasound) indicate continuous blood regeneration within the heart

It is theorized that this regeneration is not purely biological, but a result of **divine intervention**, allowing the entity to sustain viability despite ongoing extraction.

---

As of 2012, surplus Pureblood has been distributed anonymously to religious institutions under the designation:

**“Pure Holy Water”**

**Packaging Conditions:**

* No disclosure of composition

* No disclosure of properties
