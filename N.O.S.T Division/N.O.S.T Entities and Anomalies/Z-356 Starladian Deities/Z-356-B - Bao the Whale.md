## **GENERAL OVERVIEW**

Z-356-B, commonly referred to as Bao or “Bao the Whale,” is a Starladian Deity belonging to the Z-356 classification.

She identifies herself as the **“Goddess of the Clouds and Seas,”** indicating the existence or conceptual presence of large-scale water bodies within Starlands, despite the realm’s known structure of floating landmasses. This suggests that “seas” may exist in non-traditional forms (e.g., suspended bodies, atmospheric oceans, or dimensional layering).

Z-356-B is a high-mobility, environment-manipulating entity with control over both **water and air-based systems**.

    Manipulation of water through direct mental control

    Manipulation of air, including compression and solidification
- **Aerial Swimming:**  
    Ability to “swim” through air as if it were water

    Air can be compressed into tangible forms for movement or force application
- **Flight / Hovering:**

- **Enhanced Strength:**  
    Likely augmented through pressure manipulation and fluid reinforcement
