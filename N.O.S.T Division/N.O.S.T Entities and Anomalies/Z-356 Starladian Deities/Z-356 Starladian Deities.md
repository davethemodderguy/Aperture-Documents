## **GENERAL OVERVIEW**

Z-356 designates a class of high-tier entities originating from Starlands, commonly referred to as **“Starladian Deities.”**

Unlike Z-314 (Dawnbringers), Z-356 entities are **not part of the Dawnbringer lineage**, and have no direct hierarchical, familial, or structural connection to Z-314-C (Charlie Dawnbringer) or associated units.

Instead, Z-356 entities appear to represent **conceptual or domain-based embodiments**, each governing or aligning with a specific aspect of existence within Starlands (e.g., nature, time, decay, light, etc.).

These entities are historically referred to as “gods” by Starladian inhabitants.

---

## **STRUCTURAL DIFFERENCES — Z-356 vs Z-314**

**Z-314 (Dawnbringers):**

* Guardian / enforcer roles  
* Structured hierarchy  
* Direct involvement in maintaining Starlands order  
* Strong interpersonal connections

**Z-356 (Starladian Deities):**

* Domain-based existence (conceptual authority)  
* No unified hierarchy confirmed  
* Operate independently of Dawnbringer systems  
* Presence tied to influence rather than governance

Z-356 entities do not replace Dawnbringers, but instead exist **parallel** to them.
