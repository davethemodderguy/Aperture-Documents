## **GENERAL OVERVIEW**

Z-356-V, known as Viridis, is a high-authority Starlands entity identified as the “Goddess of Nature.” Historical references place her as a **battle-capable archangel-class entity** active during the Great Starladian War.

The current instance of Z-356-V differs significantly from recorded descriptions.

The entity exists in a **spectral (ghost-like) form**, composed of green luminescent energy with no confirmed stable biological structure. This state appears to be the result of an incomplete or altered manifestation process, originating from a pre-existing anomalous object located within Z-314-S’s garden.

Z-356-V presents with **severe memory loss**, lacking recollection of her historical role, past conflicts, or prior associations with Dawnbringer-class entities.

---

## **MANIFESTATION ORIGIN**

Z-356-V originated from a persistent anomalous construct within Star’s garden:

* A rose composed of green luminescent energy  
* Non-physical yet spatially anchored  
* Long-term stable presence prior to transformation

On manifestation, the rose ceased to exist and reformed into the current humanoid entity.

This suggests Z-356-V was in a **dormant or sealed state** prior to emergence.
