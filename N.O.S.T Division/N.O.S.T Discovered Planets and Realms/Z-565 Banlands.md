II. ENVIRONMENTAL OVERVIEW

The Banlands present a stark contrast to the Starlands.

Primary characteristics include:

* A hostile, high-temperature environment  
* Persistent volcanic and infernal terrain formations  
* Atmospheric instability with unknown particulate composition

Surrounding the primary landmass is a vast anomalous body:

**Z-565-A — “Ocean of Souls”**

Description:

* A non-liquid metaphysical expanse composed of accumulated soul entities  
* Functions as a containment layer for deceased consciousness  
* Serves as a staging ground for post-mortem processing

Documented Process:

**Treeification** — the absorption of soul entities into a central growth structure (“Tree of the Banlands”) for unknown energetic or ecological purposes.

VI. FUNCTIONAL SYSTEMS OF Z-565

The Banlands do not operate as a purely punitive afterlife environment.

Instead, a structured cycle exists:

1. Souls enter the **Ocean of Souls (Z-565-A)**  
2. Evaluation or passive holding state occurs  
3. Outcomes include:  
   * **Revival** (conditional)  
   * **Treeification** (default terminal process)

Agreement Terms with Aperture:

* Personnel unrecoverable from Starlands operations may be **exempt from immediate treeification**  
* Eligible individuals are reassigned for revival processing
